Greetings from Lawrence "Larry" Rector, CPA!

As a leader like you, I've experienced many moments where the path forward looked unclear. During my years of leading or helping others, we have overcome many scenarios. Sometimes it's:

  • Your company is on a downward trajectory needing a path to recovery and stability.
  • Many lucrative opportunities are within reach, but an obstacle is keeping it out of your reach.
  • Due to an array of complexities, next steps are surrounded in a fog blocking your view.
  • It's too complicated to be an expert in everything, so collaborating with a trusted person will lighten the load.

In addition to owning and running Rector Business Solutions, LLC, I am proud of the opportunities I've had working alongside many amazing people.

I served as the COO/CFO of Dayspring Health, Inc. Here I oversaw the financial and operational activities of the organization in both Kentucky and Tennessee. While there, I directed an expansion of revenue sources, enhanced patient experience outcomes, opened a comprehensive regional health center, and an addition of clinical services.

In addition, I provided consulting services and later was hired to serve as the Vice President of Finance at Cherokee Health Systems in Tennessee.

Prior to joining Dayspring Health, Inc., I served as a financial operations leader for various organizations including University of the Cumberlands, University of North Carolina Charlotte, and Johnson University.

I am a licensed CPA in Kentucky and am a member of the AICPA and KyCPA societies of CPAs. I also earned my MBA from the University of the Cumberlands.

Lastly, when away from the office, I enjoy family time with my wife Amy and four children, or spending time reading, running, volunteering at church, or completing house projects.
